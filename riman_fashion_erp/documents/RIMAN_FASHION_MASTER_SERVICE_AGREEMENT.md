## ARTICLE 2: SERVICES PROVIDED

### 2.1 Rental of Ready-Made Dresses

**(a)** Riman offers a curated collection of ready-made dresses available for rental for a specific period mutually agreed upon by the parties in advance.

**(b)** The Client may reserve a Dress by paying the specified Deposit amount as outlined in Article 3 (Payment Terms).

**(c)** Upon payment of the Deposit, Riman shall hold the selected Dress for the Client pending completion of payment of all required amounts.

**(d)** Rental periods shall be clearly specified in the invoice/contract, including exact pickup and return dates and times.

### 2.2 Sale of Ready-Made Dresses

**(a)** The Client may purchase ready-made dresses from the Riman Fashion collection at prices mutually agreed upon prior to purchase.

**(b)** The final price shall include the cost of the Dress, applicable VAT, and any alterations or customizations requested.

**(c)** Upon full payment, ownership of the Dress transfers to the Client, and Riman bears no further responsibility for the Dress.

### 2.3 Design and Manufacturing of Custom-Made Dresses

**(a)** Riman shall design and manufacture custom dresses based on either:
   - (i) An existing design from the Riman collection, adapted to Client specifications; or
   - (ii) An original design created according to the Client's specific requirements and preferences.

**(b)** Custom-made dresses may be purchased outright or rented for the first wear (or subsequent use) following manufacture.

**(c)** The design process shall include:
   - (i) Initial consultation and design approval;
   - (ii) Material selection;
   - (iii) At least one (1) Prova (fitting session);
   - (iv) Final adjustments based on Prova feedback;
   - (v) Production and delivery on the agreed date.

**(d)** Riman shall communicate all design developments, material selections, and timeline changes to the Client in a timely manner.

**(e)** Design timelines shall be provided at the time of order and shall be based on Riman's assessment of technical and production capabilities.

## ARTICLE 3: PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Payment for Rental of Ready-Made Dresses

**(a)** **Deposit:** Upon booking a rental Dress, the Client shall pay a Deposit equal to **25% (one-quarter)** of the total rental value. This Deposit is **non-refundable** and serves to reserve the Dress.

**(b)** **Balance Payment:** The remaining balance, plus the Security Deposit amount (as specified in Article 3.3), shall be paid in full upon receipt/pickup of the Dress.

**(c)** **Payment Method:** Payment shall be made via [cash / bank transfer / credit card / other: ______________].

**(d)** All payments must be received by Riman prior to the Delivery Date, except where a written payment plan has been agreed in writing by both parties.

### 3.2 Payment for Custom-Made Dresses

**(a)** **Advance Payment:** Upon placement of the order for a custom-made Dress, the Client shall pay **50%** of the total Dress value as an advance payment.

**(b)** **Final Payment:** The remaining **50%** shall be paid upon delivery of the completed Dress to the Client.

**(c)** **Payment Schedule:** The agreed payment schedule and due dates shall be specified in writing at the time of order.

**(d)** **Price Adjustments:** In the event of material price increases, design modifications requested by the Client, or unforeseen production costs, Riman shall notify the Client in writing and may request an adjustment to the total price, subject to Client approval.

### 3.3 Security Deposit

**(a)** A Security Deposit amount (to be specified in the invoice) shall be collected when renting any Dress, whether ready-made or newly tailored.

**(b)** The Security Deposit shall be refunded to the Client within **[5-7 Business Days]** following return of the Dress in Good Condition on or before the agreed Return Date.

**(c)** If the Dress is damaged or is not returned by the agreed Return Date, Riman may deduct from the Security Deposit:
   - (i) The cost of repairs for any damage;
   - (ii) Late fees as specified in Article 5.1(a);
   - (iii) The full value of the Dress in case of irreparable damage or loss.

**(d)** Any remaining balance of the Security Deposit, after deductions, shall be refunded as specified in Section 3.3(b).

### 3.4 Taxes and Additional Fees

**(a)** All quoted prices exclude **Value Added Tax (VAT) at the rate of 5%**, which shall be added to the invoice total and is the responsibility of the Client.

**(b)** Any additional fees for alterations, rush orders, special requests, or delivery to non-standard locations shall be quoted separately and must be approved by the Client in writing before services are rendered.

## ARTICLE 5: LATE RETURN AND DAMAGE PROVISIONS

### 5.1 Late Return Penalties

**(a)** **Daily Late Fee:** For each day (or partial day) that the Dress is not returned by the agreed Return Date, the Client shall pay a late fee equal to **10% of the daily rental value**, calculated as (Total Rental Value ÷ Number of Rental Days) × 10%.

**(b)** **Example Calculation:** If the rental value is AED 500 for 5 days (AED 100/day), the daily late fee is 10% × AED 100 = AED 10 per day.

**(c)** **Maximum Liability:** Late fees shall not exceed [50% of the original rental value] in total, unless Riman pursues additional remedies.

**(d)** **Notification:** Riman shall notify the Client of accumulated late fees on the [2nd and 5th day] of delay.

### 5.2 Condition of the Dress Upon Return

**(a)** The Client warrants that the Dress shall be returned in Good Condition, free from damage caused by the Client.

**(b)** Normal wear and tear, including minor color fading or insignificant surface marks, shall not constitute damage for which deductions are made.

**(c)** Damage such as tears, permanent stains, broken zippers, loose seams, or missing embellishments shall result in deductions from the Security Deposit.

### 5.3 Damage Assessment and Repair Procedures

**(a)** **Damage Documentation:** Upon discovery of damage, Riman shall document the damage with photographs and provide written notice to the Client within **[48 hours]** of the Dress's return.

**(b)** **Repair Cost Estimate:** Riman shall provide a written estimate of repair costs, itemized by specific damage and repair required.

**(c)** **Deduction from Security Deposit:** Repair costs shall be deducted from the Security Deposit. The Client shall be provided with a detailed statement showing all deductions.

**(d)** **Excess Damage:** If repair costs exceed the Security Deposit amount, the Client remains liable for the full cost of repairs, and Riman may invoice the Client for the excess amount within **[7 Business Days]** of final repair completion.

### 5.4 Irreparable Damage or Loss

**(a)** In the event of irreparable damage, permanent staining, significant loss of material (e.g., large tears), or total loss of the Dress, the Client shall bear and pay the **full replacement value** of the Dress to Riman.

**(b)** **Replacement Value** means the current retail value of the Dress in Riman's collection, or if the Dress is no longer available, a comparable Dress of similar quality and cost.

**(c)** The replacement value shall be invoiced to the Client, and payment is due within **[14 days]** of invoice date.

**(d)** This provision does not limit any other remedies available to Riman under this Agreement or applicable law.

**(e)** **Prova (Fitting Session):** At least one Prova session shall be scheduled [______] days before the planned Delivery Date. Additional Prova sessions may be scheduled at Client request and may incur additional fees as agreed.

### 6.2 Cancellation Policy for Custom-Made Dresses

**(a)** **Cancellation Before Production Commences:** If the Client cancels the order **before production has commenced**, Riman shall refund **80% of the Deposit paid**, retaining 20% as a cancellation fee to cover design time and administrative costs.

**(b)** **Cancellation During Production:** If the Client cancels **after production has commenced**, the deposit paid is **non-refundable**, as Riman has incurred material costs and labor.

**(c)** **Cancellation After Delivery:** Once the Dress has been completed and delivered to the Client, or if the Client has received it for Prova and approved it, **no refund is available**. The Client must pay the full balance owed.

**(d)** **Cancellation by Riman:** If Riman cancels the order due to business circumstances or inability to complete the Dress as specified, Riman shall refund all deposits paid without deduction.

**(e)** **Written Notice Required:** All cancellations must be submitted in writing (email or hand-delivered) to Riman within [48 hours] of the decision to cancel.

### 6.3 Delivery of Custom-Made Dresses

**(a)** **Delivery Date:** The Dress shall be delivered on the Delivery Date specified in the order. Riman shall make reasonable efforts to meet this date but reserves the right to request a reasonable extension (up to [7 days]) if unforeseen circumstances arise.

**(b)** **Advance Notice:** If Riman anticipates a delay beyond [7 days], the Client shall be notified in writing with the revised Delivery Date. The Client may accept the revised date or, if unacceptable, may cancel per Section 6.2.

**(c)** **Delivery Location:** The Dress shall be delivered to the address specified in the order or picked up by the Client at Riman's location, as agreed.

**(d)** **Fitting Upon Delivery:** The Client shall fit the Dress upon delivery. If adjustments are necessary, they must be communicated to Riman within **[48 hours]** of delivery. Minor adjustments (hemming, small alterations) shall be completed at no additional charge if attributable to measurement error by Riman.

### 7.1 Rental Returns

**(a)** **Non-Refundable Deposit:** The 25% Deposit paid to reserve a rental Dress is **non-refundable** in all circumstances, including if the Client cancels the rental after booking.

**(b)** **Refundable Security Deposit:** The Security Deposit is refundable upon return of the Dress in Good Condition on or before the Return Date, less any deductions for damage or late return fees.

**(c)** **Refund Timeline:** Any refundable amounts shall be returned to the Client within **[5-7 Business Days]** of return of the Dress, via the same payment method used for payment.

### 7.2 Custom-Made Dress Returns

**(a)** **Non-Refundable Deposit:** The 50% advance payment for a custom-made dress is **non-refundable**, except when Riman cancels the order.

**(b)** **No Returns After Delivery:** Upon delivery and fitting, the Customer cannot claim a refund of any amounts paid if the Dress has been manufactured to the Customer's specifications.

**(c)** **Defective Products:** If the Dress delivered is materially defective (e.g., seams not sewn, major stitching errors) and not attributable to the Client's requested specifications, Riman shall either:
   - (i) Repair the defect at no additional cost; or
   - (ii) Offer a replacement Dress; or
   - (iii) Refund the full balance owed by the Client.

The Client shall notify Riman of defects within **[7 days]** of delivery.

**(d)** **Client's Right to Refuse Delivery:** If, upon Prova, the Client determines the Dress does not meet the agreed specifications, the Client may:
   - (i) Request modifications (which Riman shall make at no additional charge if attributable to Riman's error);
   - (ii) Refuse delivery and forfeit the Deposit, or
   - (iii) Accept the Dress as-is with a partial refund negotiated in writing.
